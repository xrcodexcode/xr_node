What really hides behind the words “research shows”?  
  
We trust scientific studies to shape what we buy, believe, and even the policies that govern us. But behind that authority is a system vulnerable to fake papers and scientific fraud.  
This video investigates how bad research enters legitimate systems, and what happens when science begins rewarding numbers over truth.
