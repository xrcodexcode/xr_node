# Source — Atomic Habits

## Key Takeaways
- **1% Better Every Day**: The compounding effect of small habits leads to massive differences over time.
- **Systems over Goals**: Goals are about the results you want to achieve, but systems are about the processes that lead to those results. You fall to the level of your systems.
- **Identity-based Habits**: The most effective way to change behavior is to focus not on what you want to achieve, but on who you wish to become.
- **The Four Laws of Behavior Change**: A simple framework to build better habits (Make it Obvious, Make it Attractive, Make it Easy, Make it Satisfying) and break bad ones (Invert them).
