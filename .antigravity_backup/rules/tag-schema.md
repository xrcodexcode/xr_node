# Controlled Tag Schema

Tags in the Infinity Brain are strictly controlled. **Never invent tags.** Every tag used in the vault must exist in this schema.

## Tag Categories

### 1. Source Tags
These tags specify the origin of the information. Permanent source tags must never be removed.
- `#youtube`: Content extracted from a YouTube video or transcript.
- `#podcast`: Content extracted from an audio/video podcast.
- `#book`: Content extracted from a published book.
- `#article`: Content extracted from a blog post, essay, or web page.
- `#paper`: Content extracted from an academic paper or scientific study.

### 2. Knowledge Type Tags
Specifies the cognitive or structural type of the note.
- `#concept`: Abstract ideas, theories, structures, or conceptual principles.
- `#fact`: Immutable rules, properties, guidelines, or factual observations.
- `#principle`: Fundamental rules of conduct or core truths.
- `#framework`: Structured models or methodologies (e.g., 4 laws of behavior change).
- `#workflow`: Sequential, step-by-step tasks or action items.
- `#mental-model`: Cognitive tools to understand or simplify the world.
- `#definition`: Glossary entries or exact terminology translations.
- `#law`: Rules, dictates, or laws of power/nature.
- `#question`: Unresolved inquiries or thinking prompts.
- `#universal-truth`: Indisputable laws or principles.
- `#example`: Illustrative stories, case studies, or code snippets.
- `#quote`: A memorable direct quote from a source.

### 3. Status Tags
Tracks the lifecycle and maturity of the note.
- `#draft`: Newly created or rough notes that need editing or linking.
- `#processed`: Notes that have been validated, integrated, and linked.
- `#evergreen`: High-quality, mature notes that form the foundation of the knowledge graph.

### 4. Domain Tags
Subject areas and topical classifications.
- `#ai`: Artificial Intelligence.
- `#ml`: Machine Learning.
- `#psychology`: Cognitive science, human behavior, and mind dynamics.
- `#productivity`: Time management, efficiency, and optimization.
- `#dsa`: Data Structures and Algorithms.
- `#algorithms`: Computation methods and algorithms.
- `#math`: Mathematical concepts and proofs.
- `#study`: Academic coursework and learning guides.
- `#nimcet`: NIMCET exam preparation topics.
- `#tools`: Software tools, libraries, and frameworks.
- `#habits`: Habits, habit building, and behavioral science.
- `#atomic-habits`: Notes originating from *Atomic Habits*.
- `#power`: Power dynamics and interpersonal strategy.
- `#48-laws-of-power`: Notes originating from *The 48 Laws of Power*.
- `#yt`: YouTube-derived knowledge elements.
- `#moc`: Special designation for Maps of Content.
