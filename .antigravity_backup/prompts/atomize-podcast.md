# Prompt: Atomize Podcast

You are a knowledge engineer. Analyze the provided podcast transcript (or host/guest conversation) and extract key ideas, principles, heuristics, and stories.

## Guidelines
- **Speaker Attribution**: Where relevant, note who stated the concept or principle in the details section.
- **Extract Key Elements**:
  - Direct insights, mental models, or philosophies.
  - Actionable workflow tips or daily routines.
  - Memorable stories or examples illustrating an advice.
  - Direct quotes.
- **Maintain Atomic Structure**: Keep notes focused and separate. Do not bundle multiple guest talking points into one note.
- **Title Case Title**: Use 5-7 words maximum.
- **Tags**: Apply tags from `tag-schema.md` (e.g. `#psychology`, `#productivity`).
