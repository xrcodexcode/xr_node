# Precision Castparts Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired Precision Castparts Corp. (PCC) in 2016 for $37.2 billion, representing one of the largest acquisitions in Berkshire's history. PCC is a leading manufacturer of complex metal parts for aerospace engines and power turbines.

## Why it matters / connection
PCC enjoys an immense economic moat based on high switching costs. Its parts require rigorous regulatory certification, making it extremely difficult for aerospace clients (like Boeing or Airbus) to switch suppliers. Despite pandemic-era aerospace declines, it represents a durable, capital-intensive asset.
