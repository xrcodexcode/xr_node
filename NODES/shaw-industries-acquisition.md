# Shaw Industries Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired Shaw Industries, the world's largest carpet manufacturer, in stages starting in 2001. Shaw is a leader in residential and commercial flooring.

## Why it matters / connection
Shaw operates in a low-margin, highly competitive commodity industry, but it enjoys a cost-advantage moat driven by immense scale and vertical integration. Buffett backed Shaw because of its scale efficiency and excellent operational management under Bob Shaw.
