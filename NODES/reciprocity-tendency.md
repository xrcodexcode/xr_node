# Reciprocity Tendency

## Core idea
Reciprocity Tendency is the hardwired human impulse to return favors, gifts, or concessions received from others. It is one of the primary psychological influences identified by Charlie Munger in human misjudgment.

## Why it matters / connection
In corporate negotiations and purchasing, salespeople exploit reciprocity by offering free lunches, gifts, or minor concessions to prompt buyers to accept overpriced deals. Munger and Buffett manage this by establishing strict rules, such as minimizing face-to-face deal negotiations and buying via bids.
