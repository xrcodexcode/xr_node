# Starlink Business Strategy

SpaceX's satellite internet constellation designed to fund Mars colonization by generating $30B+/year in revenue.

## Explanation

Starlink is a constellation of thousands of low-Earth-orbit satellites providing high-speed, low-latency internet globally. It serves customers in remote areas with no broadband access. As of 2025, it has over 4 million subscribers.

The strategic logic: the global telecom market is ~$1T/year. If Starlink captures 3-5%, that's $30-50B/year — enough to fund SpaceX's Starship development and Mars colonization. Musk explicitly stated: "Starlink is the key to generating the revenue needed to build a city on Mars."
