# US Air Investment Mistake

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway invested $358 million in US Air preferred stock in 1989. The investment suffered from massive operational losses due to labor disputes and industry-wide fare wars.

## Why it matters / connection
Buffett later described this as a major mistake driven by ignoring the structural economics of the airline industry. Airlines are capital-intensive, highly leveraged, and sell a commodity service without pricing power, showing the danger of investing in businesses without durable economic moats.
