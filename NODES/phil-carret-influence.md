# Phil Carret Influence

## Core idea
Philip Carret was a legendary investor and founder of the Pioneer Fund (1928), one of the first mutual funds in the United States. He was a close friend and early role model for Warren Buffett, who praised Carret's long-term investment record.

## Why it matters / connection
Carret demonstrated that a buy-and-hold strategy with extremely low portfolio turnover could beat the market over decades while minimizing capital gains taxes. His discipline in buying good businesses and holding them for the long term served as a blueprint for Buffett's compounding strategy.
