# Make Money While You Sleep

## Core idea
Warren Buffett advised, 'If you don't find a way to make money while you sleep, you will work until you die.' This highlights the difference between active labor income and passive capital compounding.

## Why it matters / connection
Achieving financial independence requires owning assets (such as equities, businesses, or real estate) that generate cash flow and grow in value independently of the owner's labor hours. This principle is the foundation of value investing and long-term wealth compounding.
