# Use Absence to Increase Respect and Honor

## Core idea
Too much circulation makes the price go down: The more you are seen and heard from, the more common you appear. If you are already established in a group, temporary withdrawal from it will make you more talked about, even more admired. You must learn when to leave. Create value through scarcity.

## Why it matters / connection
Deioces, a man of great reputation in Media, refused to serve as judge anymore because the work took up too much of his time. Without his arbitrations, the country fell into lawlessness. The people begged him to return, and he agreed only on the condition that they build him a palace and crown him king. By creating scarcity through his absence, he elevated his value and secured absolute power.
