# Howard Buffett's Influence

## Core idea
Howard Homan Buffett was Warren Buffett's father, a US Congressman from Nebraska, and a stockbroker who instilled Warren's core values of moral independence and financial discipline.

## Why it matters / connection
Howard taught Warren to live according to an 'Inner Scorecard'—relying on one's own standards rather than the opinions of others. This moral independence allowed Warren to resist Wall Street herd behaviors, forming the psychological foundation of his contrarian investment style.
