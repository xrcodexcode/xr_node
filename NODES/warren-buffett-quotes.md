# Selected Warren Buffett Quotes

## Core idea
Warren Buffett is famous for his pithy, memorable summaries of complex investment, business, and life principles. These quotes serve as heuristics for decision making and emotional discipline.

## Key Quotes & Explanation

### On Temperament
> "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful."
- **Meaning**: The stock market is driven by cycles of fear and greed. A rational investor acts contrarian to profit from market mispricings rather than getting swept up in emotional panics or bubbles.

### On Business Quality & Pricing Power
> "It's far better to buy a wonderful company at a fair price than a fair company at a wonderful price."
- **Meaning**: High-quality businesses with durable competitive advantages (moats) compound capital efficiently over time. Buying them at a reasonable price yields better results than buying struggling companies at very cheap prices.

### On the Circle of Competence
> "Risk comes from not knowing what you're doing."
- **Meaning**: Staying strictly within one's circle of competence reduces investment risk. Risk is not a function of price volatility, but of operating in ignorance.

### On Long-Term Thinking
> "Someone's sitting in the shade today because someone planted a tree a long time ago."
- **Meaning**: Immense wealth and success are the result of compounding decisions made decades prior. It requires patience and long-term planning.
