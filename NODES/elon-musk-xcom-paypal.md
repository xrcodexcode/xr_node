# Elon Musk X.com PayPal

Musk co-founded X.com in 1999, an online bank that merged with Confinity to become PayPal. Sold to eBay in 2002 for $1.5 billion, making Musk a billionaire.

## Explanation

In 1999, after Zip2, Musk co-founded X.com with the vision of a "full-service online bank" — checking, savings, investments all digital with no physical branches. He was ousted as CEO in 2000 over disagreements about technology (he wanted to migrate off the Unix-based PayPal backend, which engineers resisted). However, X.com had already defined the product direction.

The merged company (X.com + Confinity) was renamed PayPal in 2001. It became the dominant payment system on eBay. In October 2002, eBay acquired PayPal for $1.5 billion in stock. Musk, as the largest shareholder (~11.7%), received ~$165-180 million.

## Why it matters

PayPal gave Musk the capital to fund SpaceX and Tesla. He invested $100M in SpaceX, $70M in Tesla, and $10M in SolarCity. Without PayPal, neither company would have been possible. The PayPal mafia (Thiel, Levchin, Hoffman, etc.) also became a powerful network.
