# Two Slot Punch Card Heuristic

## Core idea
The Two Slot Punch Card is an extreme version of the lifetime punch card heuristic, where an investor behaves as if they can only make two major investment decisions in their entire career.

## Why it matters / connection
This cognitive constraint eliminates portfolio churn and trading costs. By forcing the investor to act only when they have absolute conviction and structural advantages, it prevents average or low-conviction investments, concentrating capital on the highest-value opportunities.
