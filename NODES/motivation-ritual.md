# Motivation Ritual

## Core idea
A Motivation Ritual is a method where you associate a difficult habit with something you highly enjoy, executing a short, pleasurable routine immediately before starting the hard task.

## Why it matters / connection
This method utilizes classical conditioning. By consistently performing a small ritual (like playing a favorite song, stretching, or drinking a specific tea) before working, your brain learns to associate the ritual's positive emotional state with the work. This momentum makes it easier to begin the difficult task. For example, athletes use structured pre-game warm-ups to enter a flow state, shifting their focus away from anxiety toward automatic, high-performance execution.
