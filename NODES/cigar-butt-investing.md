# Cigar Butt Investing

## Core idea
Cigar Butt Investing is a value strategy focused on buying mediocre, declining companies at extremely cheap prices (below liquidation value), hoping to capture 'one last free puff' of profit before they fold.

## Why it matters / connection
This was Benjamin Graham's primary strategy, which Buffett practiced early in his career. However, as Berkshire's capital scaled, this approach became unworkable because declining businesses tie up cash and require constant restructuring. It was eventually replaced by Munger's focus on high-quality compounding businesses.
