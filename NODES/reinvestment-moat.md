# Reinvestment Moat vs Legacy Moat

## Core idea
A Reinvestment Moat is a competitive advantage that allows a business to reinvest its surplus earnings internally at high rates of return, whereas a Legacy Moat protects high returns on existing capital but lacks organic growth opportunities.

## Why it matters / connection
See's Candies is a classic Legacy Moat—it generates massive cash but has limited capacity to expand. A Reinvestment Moat (like BNSF Railway or digital platforms) is superior because it compounds capital internally, eliminating the double taxation and search costs of external capital allocation.
