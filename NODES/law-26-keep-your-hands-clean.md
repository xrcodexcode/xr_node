# Keep Your Hands Clean

## Core idea
You must seem a paragon of civility and efficiency: Your hands are never soiled by mistakes and nasty deeds. Maintain such a spotless appearance by using others as scapegoats and cat's-paws to disguise your involvement.

## Why it matters / connection
Cesare Borgia hired the ruthless Remirro de Orco to pacify the chaotic region of Romagna. Once de Orco had executed and tortured the populace into submission, Borgia had de Orco executed and his body cut in half in the public square, distancing himself from the brutality and winning the people's favor. Cleopatra similarly used her generals and agents to carry out assassinations, preserving her divine royal image.
