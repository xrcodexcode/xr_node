# Reputation Protection Heuristic

## Core idea
The Reputation Protection Heuristic is the strict guideline that any action that could damage corporate or personal reputation must be avoided, even if the action is legal and highly profitable.

## Why it matters / connection
During the 1991 Salomon Brothers treasury bidding scandal, Buffett took over as Chairman and told employees: 'Lose money for the firm, and I will be understanding; lose a shred of reputation for the firm, and I will be ruthless.' This heuristic preserves the trust that makes Berkshire a preferred buyer for family-owned firms.
