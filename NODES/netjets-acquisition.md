# NetJets Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire acquired NetJets, a pioneer in fractional aircraft ownership, in 1998 for $725 million. NetJets allows customers to purchase a fraction of an aircraft, providing flight access without the full cost of ownership.

## Why it matters / connection
NetJets requires substantial capital to purchase its fleet, but it enjoys a scale-based economic moat. Its massive fleet size allows it to position planes more efficiently than competitors, providing superior availability and safety standards that smaller operators cannot match.
