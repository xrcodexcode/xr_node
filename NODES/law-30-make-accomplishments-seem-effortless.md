# Make Your Accomplishments Seem Effortless

## Core idea
Your actions must seem natural and executed with ease. All the toil and practice that go into them, and also all the clever tricks, must be concealed. When you act, act effortlessly, as if you could do much more. Avoid the temptation of revealing how hard you work—it only raises questions. Teach no one your tricks or they will be used against you.

## Why it matters / connection
Harry Houdini went to great lengths to hide the physical effort and mechanical secrets behind his escapes. He performed with a calm, smiling expression, making the impossible look like a matter of sheer willpower or magic. When people see the effort behind a feat, the illusion is broken and the mystery is replaced by mere labor.
