# Habit Stacking Method

## Core idea
Habit Stacking is a productivity method that involves pairing a new habit with an existing daily habit. The formula is: 'After [CURRENT HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT].'

## Why it matters / connection
This method leverages the neurological momentum of established routines. By anchoring a new, unfamiliar behavior to a deeply ingrained habit (like brushing teeth or drinking coffee), you bypass the friction of starting from scratch, making the cue obvious and the response easy to execute.
