# Loop Engineering

## Core idea
Loop Engineering is the practice of designing automated, iterative systems that prompt and orchestrate AI agents, replacing the human as the direct prompter. Instead of a human manually typing prompts back and forth to refine an output, a programmatically defined loop manages the trigger, action, and stop condition.

## Why it matters / connection
As AI capabilities grow, human-in-the-loop workflows become a bottleneck. Loop engineering shifts the human's role from low-level prompting (micromanagement) to high-level system design (defining rubrics, grading metrics, and success criteria). This approach allows agents to iterate autonomously toward a goal, significantly increasing the quality of the final output. It represents a major transition from static prompt engineering to dynamic agent architecture.
