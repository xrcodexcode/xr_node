# Susan Thompson Buffett's Influence

## Core idea
Susan Thompson Buffett was Warren Buffett's first wife, a director of Berkshire Hathaway, and a major philanthropist who significantly shaped Warren's personal growth and views on wealth distribution.

## Why it matters / connection
Susan introduced Warren to civil rights issues and directed his attention to active philanthropy. Although they lived separately in later years, she remained his closest confidante until her death in 2004, leaving her estate to fund the Susan Thompson Buffett Foundation.
