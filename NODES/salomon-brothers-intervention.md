# Salomon Brothers Crisis Intervention

## Core idea
Warren Buffett stepped in as interim Chairman of Salomon Brothers in 1991 following a scandal where the bank's bond traders submitted illegal bids at US Treasury auctions.

## Why it matters / connection
The scandal threatened to bankrupt Salomon if the US Treasury banned it from bidding. Buffett used his credibility to convince regulators that Salomon was cooperating, saving the firm and demonstrating the high financial value of personal integrity and reputation protection during a corporate crisis.
