# Price is What You Pay Value is What You Get

## Core idea
This principle states: 'Price is what you pay. Value is what you get.' Price is dictated by market supply and demand, while value is the present value of future cash flows.

## Why it matters / connection
Understanding the difference between price and value prevents investors from overpaying during market highs, guiding them to buy assets at a discount.
