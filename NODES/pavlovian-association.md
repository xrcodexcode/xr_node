# Pavlovian Association in Branding

## Core idea
Pavlovian Association is a psychological conditioning mechanism where a brand name is repeatedly associated with positive emotions (such as happiness, joy, or trust) until the brand itself triggers those emotions in consumers.

## Why it matters / connection
Coca-Cola's marketing (associating the drink with holidays, youth, and sports) is a classic execution of this conditioning. The positive emotional association creates immense consumer loyalty and pricing power, preventing competitors from displacing it with lower-priced generic sodas, forming a strong moat.
