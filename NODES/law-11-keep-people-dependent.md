# Learn to Keep People Dependent on You

## Core idea
To maintain your independence you must always be needed and wanted. The more you are relied on, the more freedom you have. Make people depend on you for their happiness and prosperity and you have nothing to fear. Never teach them enough so that they can do without you.

## Why it matters / connection
Otto von Bismarck became Prussian prime minister by making himself indispensable to King William I. Whenever the King threatened to act against Bismarck's wishes, Bismarck would threaten to resign, which terrified the King because he could not govern without him. Henry Kissinger similarly positioned himself as the sole negotiator for crucial cold war foreign policies, ensuring his position remained secure under Richard Nixon.
