# Conceal Your Intentions

## Core idea
Keep people off-balance and in the dark by never revealing the purpose behind your actions. If they have no clue what you are up to, they cannot prepare a defense. Guide them far enough down the wrong path, envelop them in enough smoke, and by the time they realize your intentions, it will be too late.

## Why it matters / connection
The Marquis de Sévigné was advised by Ninon de Lenclos on how to seduce a countess. Ninon had the Marquis act as a friend, talk of other women, and show indifference, which confused the countess and eventually led her to fall for him. In politics, Otto von Bismarck concealed his real goal of Prussian hegemony behind declarations of peace, catching his rivals unprepared for his sudden, decisive maneuvers.
