# Bill Gates Friendship

## Core idea
Warren Buffett and Bill Gates, the co-founder of Microsoft, have maintained a close friendship since meeting in 1991. Despite operating in different sectors (tech vs. value investing), they bonded over their shared focus on rational thinking and systems design.

## Why it matters / connection
Their friendship led directly to the creation of the Giving Pledge in 2010. Furthermore, Buffett pledged the bulk of his fortune to the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, trusting their analytical approach to global philanthropy to maximize the societal return on his capital.
