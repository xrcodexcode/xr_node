# Blue Chip Stamps Investment

## Core idea
Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger took control of Blue Chip Stamps in the early 1970s. Blue Chip stamps were loyalty stamps given by retailers to customers, who would collect them and redeem them for merchandise.

## Why it matters / connection
Like insurance companies, Blue Chip Stamps collected money upfront for stamp sales and redeemed them much later, generating a large cash 'float'. Buffett and Munger reinvested this float to buy See's Candies in 1972 and a controlling interest in Wesco Financial, showing the power of float-led acquisitions.
