# Know Who You're Dealing With — Do Not Offend the Wrong Person

## Core idea
There are many different kinds of people in the world, and you can never assume that everyone will react to your strategies in the same way. Deceive or outmaneuver some people and they will spend the rest of their lives seeking vengeance. They are wolves in lambs' clothing. Choose your victims and opponents carefully, then—never offend or deceive the wrong person.

## Why it matters / connection
The Shah of Khwarizm made the mistake of insulting and executing ambassadors sent by Genghis Khan, treating them as petty merchants. In response, Genghis Khan unleashed the Mongol horde, completely destroying the Khwarizm Empire, burning cities, and salt-plowing the fields. The Shah did not understand the opponent he was dealing with, leading to his absolute annihilation.
