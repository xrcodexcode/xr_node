# Ted Weschler Successor

## Core idea
Ted Weschler is a key investment manager at Berkshire Hathaway. He was hired in 2011 to manage a portion of Berkshire's equity portfolio alongside Todd Combs, preparing the firm's capital allocation succession.

## Why it matters / connection
Weschler was recruited after anonymously winning two charity auctions to dine with Buffett, paying over $2.6 million each time. Before joining, Weschler ran Peninsula Capital, achieving exceptional long-term returns under a value framework, making him a critical successor for Berkshire's stock portfolio.
