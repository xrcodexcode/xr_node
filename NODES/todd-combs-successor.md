# Todd Combs Successor

## Core idea
Todd Combs is a major investment manager at Berkshire Hathaway. Hired in 2010, Combs manages billions of dollars of Berkshire's equity portfolio and serves as a key successor for Buffett's investment role.

## Why it matters / connection
Combs was hired based on his outstanding value investing performance at Castle Point Capital. He works alongside Ted Weschler, investing Berkshire's capital according to the same margin of safety principles as Buffett, and has also taken on operational roles, such as CEO of GEICO.
