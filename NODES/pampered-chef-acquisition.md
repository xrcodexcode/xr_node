# Pampered Chef Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire acquired Pampered Chef, a direct seller of high-quality kitchen tools and gadgets, in 2002. Founded by Doris Christopher in 1980 with a $3,000 loan, the business operates through a network of independent consultants who host cooking shows.

## Why it matters / connection
Pampered Chef is an asset-light business with high return on equity (ROE) and very low capital intensity. Buffett backed the firm's direct-selling model because it required almost no capital expenditure from the parent to scale, while generating highly predictable, high-margin cash flow.
