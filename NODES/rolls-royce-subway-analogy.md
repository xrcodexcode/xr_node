# Rolls-Royce and Subway Analogy

## Core idea
Warren Buffett famously remarked, 'Wall Street is the only place that people ride in a Rolls-Royce to get advice from those who take the subway.'

## Why it matters / connection
This analogy exposes the core conflict of interest in the financial advisory industry, where brokerage firms profit from high transaction volumes and advisory fees rather than actual investment performance. It warns investors to ignore broker hype and rely on their own independent business analysis.
