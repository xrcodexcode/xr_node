# Tom Murphy Influence

## Core idea
Thomas Murphy was the legendary CEO of Capital Cities/ABC. Buffett has repeatedly described Murphy as the finest business manager he has ever met, serving as a key mentor in operations and capital allocation.

## Why it matters / connection
Murphy pioneered the 'lean corporate headquarters' model, managing a massive media empire with minimal bureaucracy and maximum delegation to local operations. This extreme delegation combined with centralized capital allocation served as the direct blueprint for Berkshire Hathaway's corporate structure.
