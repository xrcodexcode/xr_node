# Sanborn Map Company Investment

## Core idea
Warren Buffett invested partnership capital in Sanborn Map Company in 1958, initiating his first major shareholder activism campaign.

## Why it matters / connection
Sanborn mapped US cities for insurance firms. While its core business was declining, it held a stock portfolio worth $65 per share, while Sanborn's stock traded at $45. Buffett bought a blocking stake, forcing the company to spin off the stock portfolio, yielding a significant return.
