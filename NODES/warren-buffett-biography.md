# Warren Buffett Biography

## Core idea
Warren Buffett is a legendary American investor, business magnate, and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway. Known as the "Oracle of Omaha," he built his fortune through value investing and rational capital allocation, compounding Berkshire Hathaway's book value by over 20% annually for over half a century.

## Why it matters / connection
Buffett is historically significant because he proved that a disciplined, long-term value-based investment methodology can consistently beat the stock market over decades. His success converted a failing textile mill into a massive diversified holding company with hundreds of billions in equity, establishing a blueprint for corporate governance, operational decentralization, and compounding.
