# The Illusion of Equality

## Core idea
Democratic and corporate declarations of equality are masks for deep hierarchies. Behaving as if everyone is equal in a structured system is a dangerous error that offends superiors and alienates subordinates.

## Why it matters / connection
Modern organizations claim to treat everyone as equals, encouraging open feedback and casual dynamics. However, this is a dangerous facade. Power remains concentrated around a central figure or group, and a court system is always at play. If you speak to your boss as an equal, offer unvarnished criticism, or fail to observe the hierarchy, you will face swift, silent retaliation. You must decode the actual power structure and operate within it, despite any egalitarian rhetoric.
