# Get Others to Do the Work for You, but Always Take the Credit

## Core idea
Use the wisdom, knowledge, and legwork of other people to further your own cause. Not only will such assistance save you valuable time and energy, it will give you a godlike aura of efficiency and speed. In the end your helpers will be forgotten and you will be remembered.

## Why it matters / connection
Nikola Tesla was a brilliant inventor who worked for Thomas Edison and later George Westinghouse. While Tesla did the foundational intellectual and physical labor for many breakthroughs, it was Edison and Westinghouse who patented, commercialized, and took public credit for the work. Shakespeare similarly borrowed plots, characters, and even dialogue from other writers and historical texts, but his genius was in synthesising them under his name.
