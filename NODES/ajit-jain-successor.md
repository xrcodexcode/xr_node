# Ajit Jain Successor

## Core idea
Ajit Jain is the Vice Chairman of Insurance Operations for Berkshire Hathaway. Hired by Buffett in 1986, Jain has spent decades underwriting massive, complex catastrophe risks that other insurers avoid.

## Why it matters / connection
Jain built Berkshire's reinsurance division into a global powerhouse. Underwriting giant, low-probability risks (like hurricanes or earthquakes) requires massive capital backing, which Berkshire's balance sheet provides. Buffett has repeatedly called Jain's underwriting discipline irreplaceable, generating billions in float.
