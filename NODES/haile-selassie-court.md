# Haile Selassie's Court of Dependency

## Core idea
Emperor Haile Selassie of Ethiopia maintained absolute power by deliberately cultivating a court where every noble, servant, and minister was completely dependent on him personally for their status, wealth, and survival.

## Why it matters / connection
As documented in Ryszard Kapuściński's *The Emperor*, Selassie kept his ministers divided, encouraging competition and jealousy among them. He controlled all information, personally distributed favors, and ensured no subordinate could build an independent base of support. By keeping his courtiers focused on pleasing him rather than governing the country, he neutralized any potential threats to his throne, embodying the strategy of indispensable dependency.
