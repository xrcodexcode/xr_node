# Honesty is an Expensive Gift

## Core idea
Warren Buffett famously stated, 'Honesty is a very expensive gift; don't expect it from cheap people.' This heuristic highlights the rare and valuable nature of integrity in business relationships.

## Why it matters / connection
When hiring or partnering, Buffett looks for three traits: integrity, intelligence, and energy. He warns that if a person lacks the first (integrity), the other two (intelligence and energy) will ruin the business, as a smart, active, dishonest partner will exploit the firm.
