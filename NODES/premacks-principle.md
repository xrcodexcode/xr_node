# Premack's Principle

## Core idea
Premack's Principle states that a high-probability behavior (something you want to do) can be used to reinforce a low-probability behavior (something you need to do).

## Why it matters / connection
This principle is the psychological basis for temptation bundling. By allowing yourself to perform a highly desired activity only *after* completing a required habit (e.g., watching a show only while on the treadmill), you use the anticipated reward of the desired activity to motivate the friction-filled task.
