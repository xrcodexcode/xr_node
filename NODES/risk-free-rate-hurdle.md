# Risk-Free Rate Benchmark

## Core idea
The Risk-Free Rate Benchmark is the minimum rate of return required on any investment, traditionally represented by the yield on long-term US Treasury bonds, which Buffett uses as a baseline hurdle rate.

## Why it matters / connection
In finance, riskier assets are discounted using higher discount rates. Buffett, however, uses the risk-free rate as his discount rate for all high-quality businesses, arguing that if a business requires a risk premium to justify its purchase, it is outside his circle of competence and should be avoided.
