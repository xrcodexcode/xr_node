# Be Royal in Your Own Fashion — Act Like a King to Be Treated Like One

## Core idea
The way you carry yourself will often determine how you are treated: In the long run, appearing vulgar or common will make people disrespect you. For a king respects himself and inspires the same sentiment in others. By acting regally and confident of your power, you make yourself seem destined to wear a crown.

## Why it matters / connection
Christopher Columbus secured funding for his voyage from Ferdinand and Isabella by demanding noble titles, the rank of Admiral of the Ocean Sea, and 10% of all revenues from discovered lands. His bold, kingly demands convinced the monarchs that he was indeed a man of great destiny and capability. Had he begged or negotiated modestly, they would have dismissed him as a common adventurer.
