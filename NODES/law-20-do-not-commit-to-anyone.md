# Do Not Commit to Anyone

## Core idea
It is the fool who always rushes to take a side. Do not commit to any side or cause but yourself. By maintaining your independence, you become the master of others—playing sides against one another, making them pursue you, and maintaining your leverage.

## Why it matters / connection
Queen Elizabeth I of England kept all of Europe guessing by refusing to commit to marriage with any suitor (such as King Philip of Spain or the Duke of Anjou). This kept other nations from forming alliances against England, as each hoped to win her hand. Alcibiades of Athens similarly shifted his allegiance between Athens, Sparta, and Persia, keeping himself valuable and powerful by never fully committing.
