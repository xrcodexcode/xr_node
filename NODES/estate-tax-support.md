# Estate Tax Support

## Core idea
Warren Buffett has consistently advocated for the retention of the federal estate tax, arguing that a society should not allocate its economic resources based on hereditary privilege.

## Why it matters / connection
Buffett believes that dynastic wealth distribution is harmful to a meritocratic democracy, comparing it to choosing the 2020 Olympic team by selecting the eldest sons of the 1976 Olympic medalists. His tax stance aligns with his capital allocation view that resources should flow to those who deploy them most productively.
