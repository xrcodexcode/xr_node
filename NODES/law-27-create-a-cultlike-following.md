# Play on People's Need to Believe to Create a Cultlike Following

## Core idea
People have an overwhelming desire to believe in something. Become the focal point of such belief by offering them a cause, a new faith to follow. Keep your words vague but full of promise; emphasize enthusiasm over reason and clear thinking. Give your new disciples rituals to perform, ask them to make sacrifices on your behalf. In the absence of organized religion and grand causes, your new belief system will bring you untold power.

## Why it matters / connection
Franz Mesmer, the developer of 'mesmerism' (animal magnetism), created a massive following in Paris by holding mysterious salons with soft music, dim lighting, and a magnetic tub that patients touched to cure their ailments. He kept his theories intentionally vague, demanded loyalty, and made his sessions a fashionable ritual, attracting the wealthy elite and creating an intense cult of belief.
