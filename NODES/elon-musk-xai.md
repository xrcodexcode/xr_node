# xAI Founding

Musk's artificial intelligence company founded in 2023 with the mission to "understand the true nature of the universe." Competes directly with OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and Anthropic.

## Explanation

Musk co-founded OpenAI in 2015 but left in 2018. When OpenAI shifted from non-profit to for-profit and partnered closely with Microsoft, Musk grew concerned about AI safety. He founded xAI in March 2023 to build a "maximally curious" AI — one that seeks truth rather than optimizing for corporate or political objectives.

xAI's first product was Grok, a chatbot integrated into X (formerly Twitter). In 2025, xAI acquired X in an all-stock deal, consolidating Musk's AI and social media assets. The strategy: use X's massive real-time data to train superior AI models.
