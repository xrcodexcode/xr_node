# The Law of Reversal

## Core idea
Every law of power contains its own reversal—a set of circumstances where the exact opposite strategy is required. A true master of power does not apply the laws rigidly, but remains flexible and matches their approach to the specific context.

## Why it matters / connection
Greene notes that for every law (e.g., 'Never Outshine the Master'), there are times when it must be broken (e.g., when the master is falling and you must distance yourself or accelerate their decline). Rigidity is the greatest danger in strategy. By understanding the reversal of each law, you preserve formlessness and prevent others from predicting your actions.
