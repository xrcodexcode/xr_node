# Swimming Naked Heuristic

## Core idea
Warren Buffett famously observed, 'Only when the tide goes out do you discover who's been swimming naked.' This heuristic highlights how recessions expose weak financial structures.

## Why it matters / connection
During economic booms, excessive leverage and speculative models hide structural business flaws. When the credit cycle turns (the tide goes out), these leveraged firms face liquidity crises and fail, proving the value of unleveraged balance sheets and high margins of safety.
