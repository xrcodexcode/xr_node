# Berkshire Hathaway Energy

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway Energy (formerly MidAmerican Energy) is a major utility subsidiary that reinvests 100% of its earnings into energy infrastructure, particularly wind and solar power.

## Why it matters / connection
Utilities are capital-intensive but enjoy regulated monopolies. Because Berkshire pays no dividend, it can allow BHE to retain all earnings to rebuild grids and build renewable energy sources, compounding capital at predictable rates without tax leakage.
