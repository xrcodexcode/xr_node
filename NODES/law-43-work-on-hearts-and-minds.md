# Work on the Hearts and Minds of Others

## Core idea
Coercion creates a reaction that will eventually work against you. You must seduce others into wanting to move in your direction. A person you have seduced becomes your loyal pawn. And the way to seduce others is to operate on their individual psychologies and weaknesses. Play on their emotions, appeal to their self-interest and vanity. Seduce the minds and hearts of the people and they are yours.

## Why it matters / connection
Chiang Kai-shek relied on military force and coercion to control China, alienating the peasantry. Mao Zedong, in contrast, worked on the hearts and minds of the peasants by promising land reform and treating them with respect, turning them into a massive, devoted army that swept the Nationalists from power. Seduction creates willing compliance, which is infinitely stronger than forced obedience.
