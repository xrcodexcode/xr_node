# Walter Schloss Comparison

## Core idea
Walter Schloss was an legendary value investor and fellow student of Benjamin Graham. He ran a investment partnership for 47 years, achieving market-beating returns with a strict asset-based value approach.

## Why it matters / connection
Schloss represents pure, classic Graham-style value investing: buying stocks trading at a discount to book value without visiting management or using complex analysis. His record proves that simple, disciplined bargain hunting can beat the market, contrasting with Buffett's focus on brand moats and business quality.
