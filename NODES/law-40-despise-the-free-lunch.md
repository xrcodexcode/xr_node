# Despise the Free Lunch

## Core idea
What is offered for free is dangerous—it usually involves either a trick or a hidden obligation. What has worth is worth paying for. By paying your own way you stay clear of gratitude, guilt, and deceit. It is also wise to pay the full price—there is no shortcut to excellence. Be lavish with your money and keep it circulating, for generosity is a sign and a magnet for power.

## Why it matters / connection
The Spanish conquistadors made the fatal mistake of chasing easy gold in the Americas, neglecting to build domestic manufacturing or sustainable trade. This 'free' wealth caused massive inflation and ruined the Spanish economy. In contrast, the Rothschild family understood that financial power requires massive investments, paying top dollar for information and speed.
