# Power Abhors a Vacuum

## Core idea
If you do not define your own role, status, and space, others will define it for you or occupy it, leaving you powerless. You must actively shape how you are perceived and assert your presence in any social system.

## Why it matters / connection
In human systems, vacuums of power, direction, or identity are quickly filled. If you are passive, other people's ambitions and assumptions will dictate your boundaries. To prevent this, you must recreate your image, plan your trajectory, and enter action with confidence. By projecting a clear, powerful presence, you dictate the terms of engagement and prevent others from imposing their own agendas on you.
