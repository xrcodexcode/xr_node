# The Giving Pledge

## Core idea
The Giving Pledge is a philanthropic campaign founded in 2010 by Warren Buffett and Bill Gates, encouraging the world's wealthiest individuals and families to commit to giving away more than half of their wealth to philanthropic causes either during their lifetime or in their wills.

## Why it matters / connection
Buffett has pledged to donate 99%+ of his fortune to philanthropic foundations (primarily the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and foundations run by his children). This pledge highlights his philosophy of wealth—that immense personal fortunes are best used to solve societal problems rather than being passed down as dynastic wealth. The campaign has since been signed by hundreds of billionaires worldwide, altering the landscape of modern philanthropy.
