# Rule Number One

## Core idea
Warren Buffett's two rules of investing are: 'Rule No. 1: Never lose money. Rule No. 2: Never forget Rule No. 1.'

## Why it matters / connection
This principle emphasizes risk aversion and capital preservation. Because compounding interest is geometric, a 50% loss requires a 100% gain just to get back to even. By avoiding permanent capital losses through a strict margin of safety, the investor preserves the principal needed to compound wealth.
