# Fruit of the Loom Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired apparel manufacturer Fruit of the Loom in 2002 for $835 million out of bankruptcy. The company was founded in 1851 and is one of the oldest brands in the US.

## Why it matters / connection
Despite the company's financial distress due to high debt and corporate mismanagement, Buffett recognized that the brand name was highly durable. By buying the assets out of bankruptcy at a steep discount, he secured a margin of safety and allowed the company to rebuild its distribution, generating steady earnings.
