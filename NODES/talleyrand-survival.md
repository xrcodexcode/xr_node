# The Diplomatic Survival of Talleyrand

## Core idea
Charles Maurice de Talleyrand-Périgord served as a top diplomat under Louis XVI, the French Revolution, Napoleon, and the restored Bourbon monarchy. He survived every violent regime change by mastering timing, flexibility, and making himself indispensable to whoever was in power.

## Why it matters / connection
Talleyrand was a master of political timing. He knew when a regime was failing and quietly withdrew his support before the collapse, positioning himself to help the incoming power. He refused to commit to ideologies, treating politics as an amoral game of survival and state interest. His ability to charm, gather secrets, and negotiate made him indispensable to every French ruler, regardless of their political alignment.
