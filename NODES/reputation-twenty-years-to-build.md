# Reputation Durability Principle

## Core idea
Warren Buffett noted, 'It takes 20 years to build a reputation and five minutes to ruin it. If you think about that, you'll do things differently.' This principle guides Berkshire's corporate ethics and risk management.

## Why it matters / connection
Reputation is a business's most valuable asset, yet it is highly fragile. In a decentralized corporation like Berkshire Hathaway, maintaining strict ethical compliance is critical, as a scandal in one subsidiary can damage the reputation and trust of the entire holding company.
