# Use the Surrender Tactic — Transform Weakness into Power

## Core idea
When you are weaker, never fight for honor's sake; choose surrender instead. Surrender gives you time to recover, time to torment and irritate your conqueror, time to wait for his power to wane. Do not give him the satisfaction of fighting and defeating you—surrender first. By making surrender a tool of power, you turn the tables.

## Why it matters / connection
Bertolt Brecht was summoned before the House Un-American Activities Committee during the Red Scare. Instead of fighting or refusing to answer (like the Hollywood Ten, who were jailed), Brecht was extremely polite, cooperative, and answered in confusing, translated German, making him appear harmless and compliant. They released him, and he immediately flew back to Europe, free to continue his work.
