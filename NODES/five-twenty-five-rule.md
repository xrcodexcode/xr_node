# The Five-Twenty-Five Rule

## Core idea
The 5/25 Rule is a productivity method attributed to Warren Buffett's advice to his pilot, Mike Flint, on how to prioritize goals and avoid distractions.

## Why it matters / connection
The method involves listing your top 25 goals in life, circling the top 5, and treating the remaining 20 as the 'avoid-at-all-cost' list. Buffett argues that the items on the 6-25 list are the most dangerous because they are interesting enough to consume time and energy, but distract from the 5 highest-priority goals.
