# Washington Post Investment

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway invested $10 million in the Washington Post Company in 1973, acquiring a 10% stake in the newspaper publisher at a time when the market undervalued it.

## Why it matters / connection
The market valued the entire Washington Post Company at $80 million, while Buffett's business analysis showed its print monopoly and television stations were worth at least $400 million. This huge margin of safety protected the investment, compounding into billions of dollars over decades.
