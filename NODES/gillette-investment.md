# Gillette Investment

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway invested $600 million in Gillette preferred stock in 1989. The preferred stock was convertible into common shares, which Berkshire eventually did, holding a major position for decades.

## Why it matters / connection
Gillette held a dominant global market share in razor blades, giving it massive pricing power and brand loyalty (economic moat). Buffett noted that he could sleep well at night knowing that billions of men's beards grew while he slept, generating predictable, repeat customer purchases and cash flows.
