# Cesare Borgia

## Core idea
Cesare Borgia consolidated his rule in the Romagna through a combination of extreme violence carried out by an agent, followed by the public execution of that same agent to escape blame and win the populace's support.

## Why it matters / connection
The Romagna was full of lawlessness and feuding lords. Borgia appointed Remirro de Orco, a cruel and energetic man, to pacify the region. De Orco did so with swift brutality. Once order was restored, Borgia knew the people hated the cruelty. He had de Orco arrested, executed, and his body cut in half in the public square of Cesena. This spectacular gesture satisfied the people's desire for justice, distanced Borgia from the cruelty, and left the population awed and obedient.
