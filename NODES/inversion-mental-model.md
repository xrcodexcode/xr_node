# Inversion Principle

## Core idea
Inversion is a decision-making framework that involves approaching a problem from the opposite direction—specifically, identifying how to fail or achieve the worst outcome, and then systematically avoiding those actions.

## Why it matters / connection
Munger famously advises: 'All I want to know is where I'm going to die, so I'll never go there.' By identifying how a business or investment could go bankrupt (e.g., high debt, bad management, obsolete technology) instead of just focusing on how it will succeed, investors can build a strong margin of safety.
