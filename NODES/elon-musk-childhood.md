# Elon Musk Childhood

Born June 28, 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa. A difficult childhood marked by bullying, a traumatic home life, and early immersion in computers and science fiction.

## Explanation

Musk was born to Maye Musk (Canadian model/dietitian) and Errol Musk (South African engineer). Parents divorced when he was 10. He lived primarily with his father, whom both Musk and his brother Kimbal describe as emotionally abusive. At school, Musk was severely bullied until age 15, once hospitalized after being thrown down concrete stairs.

He escaped into books: he read the entire local library, then encyclopedias. Science fiction (particularly Douglas Adams, *The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy*) shaped his worldview. He taught himself programming at age 10 on a Commodore VIC-20, and at 12 sold his first game, Blastar, for $500.

## Key facts

- Born: June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa
- Parents: Maye Musk, Errol Musk
- Siblings: Kimbal Musk (brother), Tosca Musk (sister)
- First computer: Commodore VIC-20 at age 10
- First sale: Blastar game for $500 at age 12
- Diagnosed himself as having Asperger's syndrome traits
- Left South Africa at 17 to avoid mandatory military service
