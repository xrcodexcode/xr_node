# Create Compelling Spectacles

## Core idea
Striking imagery and grand symbolic gestures create the aura of power—everyone responds to them. Stage spectacles for those around you, then, full of visual resonance and radiant symbols that heighten your presence. Dazzled by appearances, no one will see what you are really doing.

## Why it matters / connection
Diane de Poitiers consolidated her influence over King Henry II of France by staging elaborate court masques and pageants where she appeared as the goddess Diana, surrounded by symbols of purity and power. This visual mythology distracted the court from her political maneuvering and solidified her position as the de facto ruler behind the throne.
