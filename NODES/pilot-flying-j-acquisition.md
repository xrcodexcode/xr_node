# Pilot Flying J Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired control of Pilot Travel Centers (Pilot Flying J), the largest operator of travel plazas and truck stops in North America, completing the 100% acquisition in 2024.

## Why it matters / connection
Travel plazas represent geographic monopolies along key highway networks. Because truck drivers require diesel, food, and showers at regular intervals, Pilot's physical locations are insulated from digital competition. This geographic moat generates highly stable, recurring cash flows.
