# Brian Clark Fingernail Biting Example

## Core idea
Hitting a habit plateau can be solved by altering the cues. In *Atomic Habits*, James Clear shares the story of Brian Clark, who cured a lifelong nail-biting habit by changing his environment.

## Why it matters / connection
Clark asked his wife to schedule him for a manicure. By investing money in keeping his nails clean, he added financial friction and pride (identity change) to nail-biting. The physical reminder of his polished nails acted as a powerful negative cue, breaking the habit loop.
