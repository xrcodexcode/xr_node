# The Futility of Gratitude

## Core idea
Gratitude has an extremely short shelf life. Reminding others of past favors or appealing to their sense of obligation is a weak strategy; people will betray you the moment their self-interest dictates a change of course.

## Why it matters / connection
In the game of power, relying on gratitude is a fatal vulnerability. When you help someone, they feel a sense of debt, which breeds quiet resentment because it highlights their weakness. Once they no longer need your assistance, they will try to escape the obligation. Instead of asking for help based on past favors, always align your request with their current self-interest, making them see immediate material benefit in helping you.
