# Buffett Foundation

## Core idea
The Susan Thompson Buffett Foundation (originally the Buffett Foundation) was established in 1964. It is a major charitable organization funded primarily by Warren Buffett's annual gifts of Berkshire Hathaway Class B stock.

## Why it matters / connection
The foundation is one of the largest private foundations in the US, focusing heavily on reproductive health, family planning, and college scholarships. It represents the structured vehicle for the Buffett family's commitment to returning their wealth to society rather than creating a dynastic family estate.
