# The Courtier Archetype

## Core idea
The courtier is a master strategist of the social landscape. Drawing from Baldassare Castiglione's *The Book of the Courtier*, this archetype is defined by extreme self-control, adaptability, and the ability to make difficult social navigation appear natural and effortless (sprezzatura).

## Why it matters / connection
A courtier must never display anger, jealousy, or raw ambition. Instead, they act as a source of pleasure and utility to the master. They yield gracefully to those above, manage their reputation with precision, and control the flow of information. The courtier understands that power is not about raw strength, but about social influence and manipulation of symbols.
