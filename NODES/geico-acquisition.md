# GEICO Acquisition

## Core idea
GEICO (Government Employees Insurance Company) was acquired by Berkshire Hathaway in 1996, converting it from a major stock holding into a wholly owned subsidiary.

## Why it matters / connection
Buffett first visited GEICO in 1951 while studying under Benjamin Graham. GEICO's direct-to-consumer model bypassed insurance brokers, giving it a permanent low-cost advantage (economic moat). This acquisition significantly expanded Berkshire's float and solidified its insurance-led structure.
