# Clayton Homes Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired Clayton Homes, the largest manufacturer of manufactured housing and mobile homes in the US, in 2003 for $1.7 billion. The company was founded by Jim Clayton in 1956.

## Why it matters / connection
Clayton Homes is vertically integrated, building homes and also providing financing to home buyers. Because of Berkshire's AAA credit rating, Clayton was able to access debt much cheaper than its competitors, giving it a powerful cost-advantage moat to expand its lending portfolio during credit crunches.
