# AmEx Salad Oil Scandal Investment

## Core idea
Warren Buffett invested 40% of his partnership capital in American Express in 1964 following the 'Salomon Salad Oil Scandal' which halved its stock price.

## Why it matters / connection
Buffett verified that the scandal (where AmEx was defrauded by fake salad oil inventory) did not damage AmEx's core charge card and traveler's check franchise. This special situation offered an attractive entry price for a high-quality brand moat.
