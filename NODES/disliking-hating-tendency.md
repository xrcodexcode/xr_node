# Disliking and Hating Tendency

## Core idea
Disliking/Hating Tendency is the psychological bias where humans ignore the virtues of people, products, or ideas they dislike, while exaggerating their faults. It leads to severe cognitive blindness.

## Why it matters / connection
In investing, this bias causes people to miss highly profitable opportunities in industries they personally dislike or find unfashionable (like oil, tobacco, or traditional retail). A rational investor remains objective and focuses on numbers.
