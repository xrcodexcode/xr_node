# Atomic Habit

## Core idea
The book defines the constituent parts of an "atomic habit" by breaking down both terms:
- **atomic**: 
  1. An extremely small amount of a thing; the single irreducible unit of a larger system.
  2. The source of immense energy or power.
- **habit**:
  1. A routine or practice performed regularly; an automatic response to a specific situation.

Combining these definitions, an **atomic habit** is a regular practice or routine that is not only small and easy to do, but is also the source of incredible power. It is an irreducible component of a larger system of compound growth.

## Why it matters / connection
Understanding habits at the "atomic" level shifts the focus away from massive, sudden transformations and highlights the power of the smallest possible actions. These tiny routines are the building blocks of remarkable results, just as physical atoms are the building blocks of complex molecules.
