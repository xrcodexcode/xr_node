# Quiet Thinking Habit

## Core idea
The Quiet Thinking Habit is the practice of dedicating long, uninterrupted blocks of time daily for reading, writing, and deep contemplation, keeping a completely open schedule free of meetings or phone calls.

## Why it matters / connection
Buffett estimates he spends 80% of his day reading and thinking. By protecting his cognitive capacity from real-time operational distractions, he can process business reports deeply and make rational decisions, highlighting that investment success is driven by temperament and thinking quality.
