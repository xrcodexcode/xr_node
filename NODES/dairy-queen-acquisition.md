# Dairy Queen Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired International Dairy Queen in 1997 for $585 million. Founded in 1940, it is a classic American fast-food chain with thousands of franchised locations globally.

## Why it matters / connection
Dairy Queen operates primarily on a franchise model, which makes it an asset-light business with high return on capital. Because franchisees fund the capital expenditures for stores, the parent company receives stable royalty fees with very low capital intensity.
