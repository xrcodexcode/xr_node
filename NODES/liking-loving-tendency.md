# Liking and Loving Tendency

## Core idea
Liking/Loving Tendency is the psychological bias where humans ignore the faults of people or products they admire, while exaggerating their virtues. It often leads to irrational trust and valuation.

## Why it matters / connection
In stock markets, this bias makes investors blind to structural flaws or excessive valuations of companies they love (e.g., tech giants or popular consumer brands). Buffett avoids this by separating product admiration from cold, cash-flow-based intrinsic value calculations.
