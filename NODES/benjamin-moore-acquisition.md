# Benjamin Moore Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired paint manufacturer Benjamin Moore & Co. in 2000 for $1 billion. Founded in 1883, it is a leading brand in premium architectural coatings.

## Why it matters / connection
Benjamin Moore possesses a strong consumer brand moat and pricing power. By selling exclusively through independent dealers rather than big-box home improvement warehouses, it maintains premium margins and customer loyalty, generating steady free cash flow.
