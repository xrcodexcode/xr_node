# Direct Communication Mandate

All employees can communicate directly with anyone, including Musk. Bypass all management chains. Formal communication channels are actively discouraged.

## Explanation

Musk sends emails telling employees: "Communication should travel via the shortest path necessary to get the job done. Anyone can email/talk to anyone else without permission." At SpaceX and Tesla, he encourages engineers to skip middle management and raise concerns directly — even to him.

He explicitly bans "chain of command" culture and "stovepipe" communication. If someone needs input from another department, they are expected to contact the relevant person directly, not go through their respective managers.

## Why it matters

This prevents information loss through management layers, speeds decision-making, and allows Musk (and others) to hear unfiltered bad news. It's a direct application of [[delete-before-optimize]] to organizational structure — delete management layers that add delay without adding value.
