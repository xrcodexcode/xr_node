# Speed of Iteration Principle

The rate at which you can iterate determines your rate of progress. Speed of iteration is a first-order competitive advantage.

## Explanation

Musk prioritizes iteration speed over all other engineering metrics. If you can test, fail, and improve in one week instead of one month, you learn 4x faster. Over five years, that compounds into an enormous advantage.

This drives concrete decisions:
- SpaceX builds and tests hardware quickly rather than spending years simulating
- Tesla pushes software updates continuously rather than bundling into annual releases
- The Boring Company dug test tunnels immediately rather than spending months planning

## Why it matters

Iteration speed is the learning rate. In rapidly evolving fields (rockets, EVs, AI), the company that learns fastest wins. Musk's organizations are designed to maximize learning cycles per unit time.
