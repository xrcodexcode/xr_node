# Lubrizol Acquisition

## Core idea
Berkshire Hathaway acquired specialty chemical manufacturer Lubrizol in 2011 for $9.7 billion. Lubrizol is a global leader in producing chemical additives for engine lubricants, driveline fluids, and industrial fuels.

## Why it matters / connection
Lubrizol operates in a highly technical niche where its additives represent a small fraction of the customer's total product cost, but are essential to performance. This creates high switching costs and pricing power, insulating the company's margins from commodity market pressures.
