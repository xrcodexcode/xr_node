# Greg Abel Successor

## Core idea
Greg Abel is the Vice Chairman of Non-Insurance Operations for Berkshire Hathaway and the designated successor to Warren Buffett as CEO. Abel previously ran Berkshire Hathaway Energy.

## Why it matters / connection
Abel has demonstrated exceptional operational management, overseeing hundreds of Berkshire's industrial, energy, and retail subsidiaries. Buffett has praised Abel's execution and capital allocation capabilities, noting he is perfectly suited to manage Berkshire's decentralized operational model.
