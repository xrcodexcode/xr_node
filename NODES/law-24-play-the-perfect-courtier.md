# Play the Perfect Courtier

## Core idea
The perfect courtier thrives in a world where everything revolves around power and political dexterity. He has mastered the art of indirection; he flatters, yields to superiors, and asserts power over others in the most oblique and graceful manner. Learn and apply the laws of courtiership and there will be no limit to your rise.

## Why it matters / connection
In *The Book of the Courtier* (1528), Baldassare Castiglione outlines the behavior required to survive and thrive at court. Courtiers must avoid being the bearer of bad news, never boast, practice 'sprezzatura' (making the difficult look easy), adapt their style to whoever they are talking to, and master the art of flattery. A courtier's survival depends on social grace and calculated behavior.
