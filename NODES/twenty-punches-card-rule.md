# Twenty Punches Card Rule

## Core idea
The Twenty Punches Card Rule is a method where an investor acts as if they have a lifetime ticket with only 20 slot punches, representing the total number of investments they can make.

## Why it matters / connection
This constraint forces extreme selectivity, preventing investors from churning portfolios and encouraging them to concentrate capital only in high-conviction ideas.
