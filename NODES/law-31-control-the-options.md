# Control the Options — Get Others to Play with the Cards You Deal

## Core idea
The best deceptions are the ones that seem to give the other person a choice: Your victims feel they are in control, but are actually puppets. Give people choices that come out in your favor whichever one they make. Force them to choose between the lesser of two evils, both of which serve your purpose.

## Why it matters / connection
Henry Kissinger would present Nixon with foreign policy options structured so that his preferred choice was the only reasonable one. He would include one extreme, aggressive option, one weak, passive option, and a balanced middle option (which he had designed). Nixon felt he was making a presidential choice, but Kissinger had controlled the options.
