# Geeks Bearing Formulas

## Core idea
Warren Buffett famously warned, 'Beware of geeks bearing formulas.' This principle cautions against over-relying on complex mathematical models to assess risk and value in finance.

## Why it matters / connection
Financial models often assume market volatility follows normal distributions, ignoring human psychology and black swan events. Buffett argues that investing requires simple arithmetic and sound business judgement, and that complex formulas often mask dangerous leverage.
