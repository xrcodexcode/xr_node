# The Boring Company Concept

A tunnel construction company founded in 2016 to solve urban traffic through underground transportation networks. Born from Musk's frustration with LA traffic.

## Explanation

Musk founded The Boring Company (TBC) in December 2016 after tweeting "Traffic is driving me nuts. I am going to build a tunnel boring machine and just start digging." The company develops faster, cheaper tunnel-boring technology using electric sleds to transport vehicles at high speed underground.

The core insight: 3D transportation (tunnels at multiple depths) can solve congestion, and current tunnel costs ($1B/km) are artificially high due to industry inefficiency — an [[idiot-index]] opportunity.
